Intro

CiderKit Pro is an app that makes it easy to calculate sugar and alcohol values in cider making. It is a standalone calculator that runs entirely on your device.

This app can also be used for winemaking, or other alcoholic drinks, but its roots are in cider making in the heart of Somerset, UK.

For features which aren't in the app already, why not submit your suggestions via the contact details and they will be considered for future updates to this app

Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 1, 2025

CiderKit Pro (“the App”) is provided by @planetguru.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) or user data. It is a standalone calculator that runs entirely on your device.

Data Usage

  • The App performs calculations (such as sugar and alcohol content per volume) locally on your device.
  • No information is transmitted to us or to any third party.

Children’s Privacy

Because the App does not collect any information, it is safe for use by individuals of all ages, including children.
